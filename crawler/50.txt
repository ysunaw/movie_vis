Catherine Deneuve (French:[katin dnv]; born 22 October 1943) is a French actress as well as an occasional singer, model and producer. She gained recognition for her portrayal of aloof, mysterious beauties for various directors, including Luis Buuel, Franois Truffaut and Roman Polanski.
