Josh James Brolin (/broln/; born February 12, 1968) is an American actor. His first role was in the 1985 film The Goonies. Since then he has appeared in a wide range of films, and is best known for his work as Llewelyn Moss in No Country for Old Men, George W. Bush in W., and Dan White in Milk, for which he received Academy Award and SAG Award nominations for Best Supporting Actor.Brolin stars as the villain Thanos in the Marvel Cinematic Universe through motion capture and voice acting. He first played the role in Guardians of the Galaxy and in a mid-credits cameo at the end of Avengers: Age of Ultron, and later became the leading villain in Avengers: Infinity War (2018). He is set to return in the untitled sequel. In April 2017, Brolin was cast as Nathan Summers / Cable as a part of a four-film contract in the X-Men film series, with his first appearance set to be in Deadpool 2.
