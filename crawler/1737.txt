David Patrick Kelly (born January 23, 1951) is an American actor and musician who has appeared in numerous films and television series. He is widely known for roles such as Luther in the cult 1979 film The Warriors, and an character named Luther in the 1982 Eddie Murphy film 48 Hrs., Sully in the 1985 Arnold Schwarzenegger film Commando, Jerry Horne in Twin Peaks, and T-Bird in the 1994 film The Crow.
