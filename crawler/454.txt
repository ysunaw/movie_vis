Michael Anthony Pea (/pnj/; Spanish:[pea]; born January 13, 1976) is an American actor and musician. He has starred in many films, such as Crash (2004), World Trade Center (2006), Shooter (2007), Observe and Report (2009), Tower Heist (2011), Battle: Los Angeles (2011), End of Watch (2012), Gangster Squad (2013), American Hustle (2013), Fury (2014), Frontera (2014), Ant-Man (2015), The Martian (2015), Collateral Beauty, CHiPs (2017) and A Wrinkle in Time (2018).
