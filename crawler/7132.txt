Vincent Philip D'Onofrio (/dnfrio/; born June 30, 1959) is an American actor, producer, and singer.He is known for his roles as Private Leonard "Gomer Pyle" Lawrence in Full Metal Jacket (1987), Wilson Fisk in Daredevil (2015-present), NYPD Detective Robert Goren in Law & Order: Criminal Intent, Edgar the Bug in Men in Black (1997), and Vic Hoskins in Jurassic World (2015). Among  honors, D'Onofrio is a Saturn Award winner and an Emmy Award nominee.
