John Paul Cusack (/kjusk/; born June 28, 1966) is an American actor, producer and screenwriter. He began acting on films during the 1980s. Cusack starred in films, including Say Anything... (1989), Grosse Pointe Blank (1997), Being John Malkovich (1999), 1408 (2007), 2012 (2009) and The Raven (2012).
