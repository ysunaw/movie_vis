Jonathan Edward Bernthal (born September 20, 1976) is an American actor best known for his television role as Shane Walsh on the AMC series The Walking Dead and as the Punisher in the Netflix series Daredevil and The Punisher as well as film roles in The Wolf of Wall Street (2013), Fury (2014), Sicario (2015), The Accountant (2016) and Baby Driver (2017).
