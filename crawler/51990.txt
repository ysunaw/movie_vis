Todd Joseph Miller (born June 4, 1981) is an American actor, stand-up comedian, producer, and writer.From 2014 to 2017, he starred as Erlich Bachman in the HBO sitcom Silicon Valley. Miller also has had starring roles in films such as Yogi Bear, Cloverfield, She's Out of My League, Big Hero 6, Deadpool, Office Christmas Party, The Emoji Movie, and Ready Player One.
