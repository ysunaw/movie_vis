Karin Konoval (/knvl/; born c.1961) is an actress who has appeared in guest starring roles in numerous TV series and supporting lead roles in many feature films. Her screen credits include Maurice the Orangutan in Rise of the Planet of the Apes, Dawn of the Planet of the Apes, and War for the Planet of the Apes; three X-Files episodes: "Clyde Bruckman's Final Repose", "Home", and "Plus One"; and the lead role of Mary Leonard in Cable Beach.
