Seann William Scott (born October 3, 1976) is an American actor, comedian and producer. His most recognized roles are Steve Stifler in the American Pie film series (1999¨C2012) and Doug Glatt in both; Goon (2011) and Goon: Last of the Enforcers (2017). He has also starred in films including Final Destination (2000), Road Trip (2000), Dude, Where's My Car? (2000), Evolution (2001), The Rundown (2003), The Dukes of Hazzard (2005), Role Models (2008) and Cop Out (2010). He voiced Crash the opossum in four theatrical films and two television specials within the Ice Age series (2006¨C2016).
