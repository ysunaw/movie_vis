Stephen Root (born November 17, 1951) is an American actor, comedian, and voice actor. He has starred as Jimmy James on the TV sitcom NewsRadio, as Milton Waddams in the film Office Space (1999), and as the voices of Bill Dauterive and Buck Strickland in the animated series King of the Hill. His  roles have included Captain K'Vada in the Star Trek: The Next Generation two-part episode "Unification" (1991), Mr. Lund in O Br, Where Art Thou? (2000), Gordon Pibb in DodgeBall (2004), and Jim Hudson in Get Out (2017).
