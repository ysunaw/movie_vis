Daniel Jason Sudeikis (/sdeks/ suu-DAY-kiss; born September 18, 1975) is an American actor, comedian and screenwriter with Lithuanian descent. In the 1990s, he began his career in improv comedy. Sudeikis performed at ComedySportz in Kansas City and with The Second City. In 2003, he was hired as a sketch writer for Saturday Night Live and was a cast member from 2005 to 2013.He appeared on the television shows 30 Rock, The Cleveland Show, Eastbound & Down, It's Always Sunny in Philadelphia, The Last Man on Earth, and  television shows.Sudeikis is known for his roles in the films Horrible Bosses (2011) and its sequel, Horrible Bosses 2 (2014), as well as  films such as Hall Pass (2011), We're the Millers (2013), Colossal (2016) and The Angry Birds Movie (2016).
