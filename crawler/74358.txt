Kelly Sheridan is a Canadian voice actress best known for being the voice for Barbie in the Barbie film series from 2001 to 2010 and from 2012 to 2015. She has also voiced in numerous cartoons and English-language dubs of Japanese anime, including as: Sango in the English dub of the anime series InuYasha, Diana Lombard in Martin Mystery, Theresa in Class of the Titans and Starlight Glimmer in My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic. She has been a member of Genus Theatre Company since August 2005.
