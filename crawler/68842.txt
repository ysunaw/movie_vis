John Cho (born Cho Yo Han; June 16, 1972) is an American actor and musician. He is best known as Harold Lee in the Harold & Kumar films; John (MILF Guy No. 2), who popularized the term "MILF" in the American Pie films; and Hikaru Sulu in the Star Trek reboot film series. Early in his career, Cho also starred in the Asian American centered films Better Luck Tomorrow (2002), Shopping for Fangs (1997), and Yellow (1998).On television, Cho played FBI agent Demetri Noh in the science fiction television drama FlashForward; Chau Presley on the sitcom Off Centre; Andy Brooks in the horror drama Sleepy Hollow, Henry Higgs in the 2014 sitcom Selfie, and Andrew Kim in The Exorcist.
