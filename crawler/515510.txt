Ryan Potter (born September 12, 1995) is an American actor and martial artist. Beginning his career as a professional actor at the age of 15, Potter is perhaps best known for his starring role as Mike Fukanaga in Supah Ninjas and for voicing Hiro Hamada in Big Hero 6 (2014). He is set to star as Garfield Logan / Beast Boy in the television series Titans, set for a 2018 release.
