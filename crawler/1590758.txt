Emily Carey (born 30 April 2003) is a British actress. She is perhaps best known for her role of Grace Beauchamp in BBC One's Casualty, which she played from 2014 to 2017.Carey also played the young Diana Prince (Wonder Woman), portrayed by Gal Gadot as an adult, in the Warner Brs movie Wonder Woman (2017). She appeared as a young Lara Croft in the Tomb Raider reboot film released in March 2018. notable work includes Mary Conan Doyle in the series Houdini and Doyle on FOX/ITV, and West End Theatre roles in Shrek The Musical at the Theatre Royal, Drury Lane, and the Olivier Award-nominated The Sound of Music at Regents Park Open Air Theatre. Carey is listed in a Huffington Post article as one of the top five child stars of 2016.Carey has signed a deal to play the title role of Anastasia in a live action Anastasia film for Netflix.
