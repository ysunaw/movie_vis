She also received recognition for starring as Rachel Dawes in the superhero film The Dark Knight (2008). She subsequently starred in the fantasy comedy family film Nanny McPhee and the Big Bang (2010), the drama Won't Back Down (2012) and the comedy-drama Frank (2014), for which she was nominated for a BIFA Award.In 2014, she made her Broadway debut in a revival of The Real Thing, and also starred in the British television BBC political spy thriller television miniseries The Honourable Woman. For her performance in the latter she won a Golden Globe Award, and was nominated for a Primetime Emmy Award.
